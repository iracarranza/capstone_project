Concert Band Program

This lesson guides students through an exploration of middle school concert band repertoire collections. Students analyze how band programs are curated, considering elements like variety, difficulty, audience engagement, and musical goals. After reviewing sample programs and discussing programming strategies, students work in groups to create and present their own hypothetical concert programs using provided repertoire lists and criteria.

Chrome Lab Oscillator

This lesson introduces students to the science of sound and the concept of pitch using Chrome Music Lab's Oscillator tool. Through guided exploration, students manipulate waveform types and frequencies to observe how sound behaves. The activity helps students connect what they hear to how sound is generated, making abstract concepts more concrete through digital interactivity.
