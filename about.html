Bio

Ira Carranza (he/she/they) is a music education major at Berklee College of Music. Ira has taught in a variety of settings, including Eleanor Van Gelder Elementary School, Bergen County Academies, and Kwong Kow Chinese School.

Originally from Bergen County, New Jersey, Ira began studying piano at age four and vocal lessons at age five. While they eventually stepped away from formal training, their deep connection to music never faded. In high school, Ira joined county and regional choirs, rekindling their passion for musical expression.

Originally pursuing a career in STEM, Ira made the decision to follow a more fulfilling path in music. Now graduating early from Berklee, they continue to grow as both an artist and educator, committed to inclusive, transformative music teaching.
