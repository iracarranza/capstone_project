Musical Analysis: Concert Band Program

In this high school-level lesson, students critically examine curated concert band programs. They explore repertoire through the lens of musical variety, technical difficulty, cultural relevance, and educational value. Students then collaborate to design their own balanced concert programs using a repertoire list and specific programming criteria, applying analytical thinking and aesthetic judgment.

Intro to Guitar: Sound Production and Technique

This lesson introduces students to the basics of guitar performance and sound production. Students explore tuning, posture, fretting, and picking techniques while building familiarity with standard chord shapes and rhythmic strumming patterns. Through hands-on learning and guided practice, students develop foundational skills that support independent exploration and performance readiness.

Grade 12 Unit Plan: Producing in Logic Pro

This unit plan supports students in developing music production skills using Logic Pro. Designed for high school seniors, the unit scaffolds key concepts such as multitrack recording, MIDI sequencing, and mixing techniques. Students complete a project-based sequence culminating in the creation of a fully produced original track.
