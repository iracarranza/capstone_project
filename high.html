Concert Band Program

This lesson engages students in exploring curated repertoire for middle school concert bands. Through analysis of sample programs, students consider factors such as musical variety, technical difficulty, audience appeal, and educational goals. Working in groups, students apply their understanding by designing and presenting their own concert programs using provided repertoire lists and specific programming criteria.

Grade 12 Unit Plan: Producing in Logic Pro
